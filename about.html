"SPORT CLUB"





The Sport Club Program at Lovely Professional University is comprised of sports clubs and are numerous and diverse. The university provides facilities for volleyball, football, basketball, swimming and many other sports.

LPU never lacks in sports opportunities or competitions, they even conduct many National and International Sports championships in which you will witness the world's finest sportsperson is competing against each other.

Lovely Professional University is sportsperson's HUB. It has sporting clubs in more than 24 different sports. It has the best trainers to properly instruct students in a safe and secure environment.
